Feynman technique

The first step of this technique is to select a topic you want to learn about. Write down everything you know about the topic. As you learn more about the topic, add it to your sheet. Once you think you understand the topic well enough, try explaining it in words a child would use. Write down your simplified explanation. If your explanation sounds confusing or isn't simple enough, you likely need to refine your knowledge. Repeat until you have a simple explanation. To test your understanding, present it to someone else. Ask yourself: "How effective was my explanation. What did they find confusing?"
