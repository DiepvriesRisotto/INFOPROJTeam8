Attention deficit

Attention deficit, often associated with Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), is a condition characterized by difficulties in sustaining focus, following through on tasks, and organizing activities. Individuals with attention deficit may be easily distracted, forgetful, and have trouble listening or paying attention to details. ADHD can also include hyperactivity and impulsivity, making it challenging to stay still or wait for one's turn. This condition can affect academic, occupational, and social functioning, requiring tailored strategies and treatments for effective management.
