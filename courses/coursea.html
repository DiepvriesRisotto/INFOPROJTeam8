SQ3R

This strategy aids reading comprehension and is helpfull for textbook material. The first step is survey. Skim the material and try to get an understanding of the main topics and ideas. The next step is question. Use questions to guide your reading. The third step is read. Look for the answers to the questions you created. The fourth step is recite. After reading, look at the questions you created and check if you can answer them. The final step is review. Conduct an overall review for maximum comprehension and memory
