Course overview

SQ3R

SQ3R is a reading comprehension method named for its five steps: survey, question, read, recite, and review.

Leitner system

The Leitner Method is designed to give the most attention to study subjects that are more challenging for a person to recall while minimizing the repetitive studying of well understood subjects.

Feynman technique

Feynman's secret lay in understanding the true essence of a concept rather than merely knowing its name, leading to his remarkable achievements. This learning technique can be applied universally, irrespective of the subject.
